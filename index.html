Herman Vandenburg Ames was anAmerican legal historian , archivist , and professor of United States constitutional history at the University of Pennsylvania and, from 1907 to 1928, dean of its graduate studies. His 1897 monograph, The Proposed Amendments to the Constitution of the United States During the First Century of Its History , was a landmark work in American constitutional history. In addition, there were also other featured works by Ames, titled John C. Calhoun and the Secession Movement of 1850 , Slavery and the Union 1845–1861 , and The XYZ Letters, the latter written with John Bach McMaster. Among his notable students were Ezra Pound , John Musser, and Herbert Eugene Bolton.      (Read more...)

About Wikipedia
Wikipedia is a collaborative, universal and multilingual encyclopedia project established on the internet under the wiki principle . Its purpose is to provide free, objective and verifiable content that everyone can edit and improve. The project is defined by the founding principles and the content is made available under the Creative Commons BY-SA license and can be reused under the same license , as long as the terms of use are respected . Anyone can publish content online as long as they create an account and follow basic rules such as verifiability or notoriety .
All Wikipedia editors are volunteers and are part of a collaborative community, without a leader, where they coordinate efforts in thematic projects and discussion spaces . Among the various help pages available are those that explain how to create an article or edit an article . If you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask . Debates and comments on articles are welcome. Discussion pages serve to centralize reflections and evaluations on how to improve Wikipedia content.
